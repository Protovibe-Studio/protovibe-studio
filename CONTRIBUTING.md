# Contributing to Protovibe

We welcome contributions! However, because Protovibe uses a dual-licensing model, we need to ensure that the project maintains the legal rights to offer commercial licenses for evil corporations.

1. **License Grant.** You grant the Protovibe project maintainers a worldwide, royalty-free, non-exclusive, perpetual, and irrevocable copyright license to use, modify, publicly display, and distribute your contributions.

3. **Original Work.** You confirm that the contribution is your original creation and you have the legal right to submit it.

By submitting your code, you confirm that you have read and accept this CLA.
